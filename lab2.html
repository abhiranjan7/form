AlphaFold and Protein Structures

  1. AlphaFold: Developed by DeepMind, an AI subsidiary of Alphabet.
  2. Function: Uses deep learning techniques to predict the 3D structures of proteins from their amino acid sequences.
  3. Importance of Proteins: Proteins are essential to almost all biological processes, and understanding their structure is crucial for understanding their function.
  4. Challenges Before AlphaFold: Determining a protein's structure was a time-consuming and complex process, often taking years.
